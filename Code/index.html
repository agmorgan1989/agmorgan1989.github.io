About Us!

A local community gym in Ebbw Vale, South Wales established in 2023, we have a core team of well qualified instructors, boasting over 30 years experience. We have everything you'd expect from a gym, including all equipment, classrooms and shower facilities. We also have a community cafe where anyone and everyone meets for mental health coffee mornings. It makes us very proud to be at the center of the community.
